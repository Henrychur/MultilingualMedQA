MultilingualMedQA

A Medical Benchmark for Multilingual Comprehension

About

The task of MultilingualMedQA is to answer medical muti-choice questions from 6 different languages. In addition, each question has a rationale for the choice. abstracts.

For more details about MultilingualMedQA, please refer to this paper:

Dataset

MultilingualMedQA is a medical muti-choice dataset of 6 different languages. It contains 45k samples for the trainset and 8,518 samples for the testset. Each question is company with a right answer and high quality rationale.
